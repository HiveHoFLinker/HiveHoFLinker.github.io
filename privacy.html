Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Discord user ID and associated server roles
  • Xbox Live gamertag (when connected)
  • Hive statistics and achievement data

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Linking your Discord and Xbox Live accounts
  • Automatic role assignment based on your Hive achievements and statistics
  • Synchronizing your Discord nickname with your gamertag
  • Processing verification requests

Data Storage & Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • All data is stored securely and not shared with third parties
  • Data is automatically removed when you use the /disconnect command
  • We implement rate limiting and other security measures to protect against abuse
  • We only store the minimum information necessary for the bot to function

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Disconnect your account using the /disconnect command
  • Request information about what data we store about you

Contact

For any privacy-related concerns or questions, you can:

  • Join our Discord server
  • Contact our moderators through Discord
